TreasureTime

Play, Read, Earn!

TreasureTime is a fun and engaging way to help kids balance screen time while developing a love for reading. Each child starts with 10 crystals every Sunday, which they can spend on screen time (1 crystal = 30 minutes) or earn more by reading books (30 minutes of reading = 1 crystal). If they save their crystals until the end of the week, they can exchange them for real pocket money (€0.50 per crystal)!

The app allows kids to track their activities by adding or removing crystals as they read or use screens. With a simple, trust-based system, they can manage their own time and develop positive habits. A weekly report helps them see their progress and calculate their final savings, making learning responsible choices both fun and rewarding!
