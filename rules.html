TreasureTime

How It Works

Every Sunday, each child receives 10 crystals. Crystals can be spent on screen time (1 crystal = 30 minutes) or earned by reading books (30 minutes = 1 crystal). Crystals saved at the end of the week can be exchanged for real pocket money (€0.50 per crystal)!

Players track their activities by adding or removing crystals when they read or use screens. The app relies on an honor system for logging activities correctly.

At the end of each week, a report is generated showing how many crystals were used, earned, or saved. The final balance determines their pocket money!
